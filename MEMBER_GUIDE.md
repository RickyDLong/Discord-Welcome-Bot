# 🤖 Welcome to the Archix Bot — Everything You Need to Know

Hey! This server runs a custom bot that tracks activity, rewards participation, and gives you a way to compete with other members. Here's everything you need to know.

---

## 📊 What Does the Bot Track?

The bot automatically tracks activity in the background — no commands needed:

- **Messages** — Every message you send earns XP and coins (once per minute to prevent spam)
- **Voice time** — Every 5 minutes you spend in a voice channel earns XP and coins
- **Reactions** — Adding reactions to messages is tracked
- **Daily streaks** — Log in and be active daily to build a streak
- **Quests** — Complete daily challenges for bonus XP

All of this feeds into your profile, the leaderboards, and the live dashboard.

## 💰 Coins & Economy

Coins are the server's currency. You earn them automatically from activity, and you can spend them in the shop or gamble them.

### Earning Coins
- Sending a message → coins
- Time in voice channels → coins per 5-minute block
- Completing daily quests → bonus coins

### Spending Coins
Use `/shop` to browse available items. Shop items include buffs that boost your XP or coin earnings for a set time period.

### Gambling
- `/economy coinflip <amount>` — 50/50 chance, doubles or loses your bet
- `/economy slots <amount>` — slot machine with varying multipliers

### Sending Coins
- `/economy give <user> <amount>` — send coins to another member

### Checking Your Balance
- `/economy balance` — see your coins, total earned, and total spent

## 🎯 Daily Quests

Every day you get a fresh set of quests to complete. These are tasks like "Send 10 messages" or "Spend 30 minutes in voice."

- `/daily` — Check your quest progress for today
- `/economy reroll` — Use a Quest Reroll buff to swap out a quest you don't want

Completing all 3 daily quests makes you a **Triple Threat** — shown on the live dashboard.

## 🛍️ Shop & Buffs

Use `/shop` to see what's available. Buffs temporarily boost your earnings:

- **XP Surge** — Bonus XP for a set period
- **Coin Magnet** — Bonus coins from activity
- **Lucky Charm** — Better odds in gambling
- **House Edge** — Stacks with other bonuses
- **Daily Double** — One-time double payout on your next daily reward
- **Quest Reroll** — Swap out one of your daily quests

Use `/boosts` to see your currently active buffs and how long they last.

## ❓ FAQ

**Do I need to do anything to start earning?**
Nope. Just chat and hang in voice. The bot tracks everything automatically.

**What's the minimum bet for gambling?**
10 coins.

**Can I lose all my coins?**
Yes — coinflip and slots can zero you out. Gamble responsibly.

**How do I check if my quest counted?**
Use `/daily` to see your current progress.

**Why didn't I get XP for that message?**
There's a 1-minute cooldown on XP per user to prevent spam. You still got coins for the message, just not XP.
